Application Programming Interface

Although not required for all stores, these APIs are a must for eCommerce development, particularly for stores that rely on their orders to be fulfilled by their suppliers to use API integrations to connect with suppliers and automate the order fulfillment process. Drop-shipping eCommerce stores also use this model to operate their business successfully.

Integration

Integrating with suppliers through APIs reduces any manual order handling and completely automates the sales fulfillment cycle.
Almost every large supplier, whether it is a manufacturer or distributor nowadays, irrespective of the product , has developed APIs. They provide access to their APIs for retailers or wholesalers to integrate and sell their products by listing them on their eCommerce store. A very popular example is Alibaba.
